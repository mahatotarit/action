Transfer Token

If your wallet is compromised by a sweeper bot and you want to transfer tokens from your compromised wallet to another wallet, use this bot.

Enter the RPC URL of your blockchain node. This URL is necessary for connecting to the blockchain network.


You can use Alchemy or Infura and other providers.

Enter the private key of the target wallet. This is the wallet to which you want to transfer tokens.

Enter your wallet's private key. This is the wallet you used to send gas fee to the compromised wallet for gas fees.

Enter the address of the token. This is the contract address of the token you want to transfer.

Enter the wallet address of the token recipient. This is the address of the compromised wallet's token recipient.
